FAQs

What is PLM in fashion?

PLM for fashion is software that allows the product lifecycle management of fashion goods such as apparel, beauty, cosmetics, footwear, accessories etc. PLM Fashion helps fashion brands manage and streamline all processes in the fashion supply chain—such as line planning, design management, product development, sampling, and so on—all the way from design to end-of-life disposal.

What is a fashion product lifecycle?

The fashion product life cycle is the journey of a fashion product from concept to discontinuation. Fashion product lifecycle management starts at the design phase, where trends are analyzed, and concepts are developed.

Next, in the development stage, prototypes are created, and materials are selected. The production phase follows, involving manufacturing and quality checks. After production, the product enters the market launch stage, where it is distributed and promoted. Finally, the decline phase occurs as demand decreases and the item is phased out, often replaced by newer styles.

What are the benefits of PLM in fashion?

PLM in the fashion industry streamlines the fashion creation process, enabling collaboration across global and/or local teams. It boosts efficiency by managing design, production, and distribution phases on a single platform, reducing time-to-market and costs. PLM for fashion industry also supports sustainability by improving material traceability and waste management.

Who can benefit from using Fashion PLM?

PLM software fashion is beneficial across various levels of the fashion industry. Designers can optimize their creative processes and material management, manufacturers gain efficiency in production planning, and retailers benefit from streamlined inventory and distribution control. What's more, brand managers use PLM software for fashion industry to ensure consistency in design language across collections, enhancing brand integrity and market reputation.

What features should I look for in an PLM for apparel industry?

When hunting for the best apparel PLM software, prioritize features like cloud-based access, real-time collaboration, robust material and inventory management, flexible design and product development capabilities, time & action tracking and open API integration with other systems. Additionally, ensure the PLM system fashion supports sustainability management and is scalable to adapt to your company's growth.

How do I implement a Fashion PLM system?

PLM technologies come in all shapes and sizes, so the implementation process begins with defining your specific needs and scope. Next, select a PLM solution tailored to the fashion industry, and plan a phased rollout that includes training for staff. Integration with existing systems is crucial, as is continuous evaluation and adaptation to ensure the PLM meets evolving business requirements.
